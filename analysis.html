Trade Analysis: AMD put options

Below is a chart of a put option contract I traded. (Put option means I went short on the stock)

Purple bars are AMD's price movement and colored bars are the put contract price movement. AMD had a weak open, and fell through a key level of support early. This caused the price to flush down further and allow the put contracts to gain value quickly.

Entry was $64 per contract, exit was $84 per contract. 31.25% gain on the trade. Left a good amount of profit on the table, but it was a scalp trade off of a key level breaking to the downside.
